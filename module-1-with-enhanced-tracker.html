🌟 Module 1 – Introduction to Vowels

Welcome to your first module! In this lesson, we’ll start by learning about vowel sounds and their importance in clear pronunciation. You'll also be introduced to the most common vowel sound in English — the "uh" sound /ə/ — which appears in almost every sentence!

📘 Focus Areas This Module

  • ✔️ Recognizing vowel sounds
  • ✔️ Understanding stressed vs. unstressed syllables
  • ✔️ Introduction to the “uh” sound /ə/

📚 This Module’s Lessons

🔹 Lesson 1 – What Are Vowels?
Learn what vowels are, how they’re formed, and why they matter in American English.

🔹 Lesson 2 – The “Uh” Sound /ə/
Discover the most common vowel in English and practice hearing and using it in words and sentences.
